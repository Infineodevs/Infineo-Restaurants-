OUR STORY

A Legacy of
Authentic Flavour

Born in Nagpur, Infineo Restaurants is a celebration of India's most treasured culinary traditions. Every dish is a journey — crafted with heirloom spices, seasonal produce, and a reverence for regional recipes that have stood the test of time.

From slow-braised kormas to fragrant biryanis, each plate is prepared with the patience and precision worthy of a royal banquet.

Our Story

WHO WE ARE

A Table Built on Tradition

Infineo Restaurants was founded on a simple conviction — that authentic Indian cuisine deserves a setting as magnificent as its flavours. For over 15 years we have welcomed guests into our dining room with warmth, reverence, and dishes that honour the full breadth of India's culinary geography.

Our chefs source heirloom spices from regional markets, cook with patience and precision, and plate with an artistry that transforms every meal into an occasion.
